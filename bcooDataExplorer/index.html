Boulder Canyon Operations Office Data Explorer

In an effort to improve service to its stakeholders, the Bureau of Reclamation's Boulder Canyon Operations Office has developed these web pages that offer hourly and daily operations data by geographic location. The data and information shown on this page are collected by an automated process and are provisional in nature. Users are cautioned to carefully consider the provisional nature of the information before using it for decisions that concern personal or public safety or the conduct of business that involves substantial monetary or operational consequences. Information concerning the accuracy and appropriate uses of these data and information may be obtained by contacting the office shown below:
Boulder Canyon Operations Office
500 Fir St.
Boulder City, NV 89005
Phone: (702) 293-8373
E-Mail: bcoowaterops@usbr.gov
